REGIONS

WELLINGTON

In Wellington, there is a Womens and a Mens league that takes place on Weekly on Sundays. This is from April - September. The National Club Champs is also held in Wellington. This is where Clubs from across New Zealand come to Wellington to a tournament. All Clubs in Wellington are open to new players even people who have never played before.

If you are interested in playing and don't have a club yet, email Jerry at jerry@gmail.com and he will set you up with the club that is the right fit for you. Below are the clubs and some information about them.

CLUBS

Victoria University

The Vic Uni Handball Club has both 2 mens teams and 1 womens team. Vic Uni is a very welcoming club that is open to players of any ages and experience. You don't have to be a student at Vic Uni to be a part of this club. Their training are weekly on thurdays usually around 8pm.

Hunters

Hunters Handball Club only has one womens team. Hunters have been the reigning champions of the Wellington Handball womens league for 2 years in a row. Hunters also were the champions of the National Club Champs in 2023. This club trains fortnightly on tuesday nights around 7pm.

Spartanz

Spartanz Handball Club have both a mens team and a womens team. Spartanz is welcoming to any new players who are keen to give handball a go. Spartanz Womens placed 2nd in the Wellington Handball League, whilst the Mens team placed 3rd. Spartanz trains on every wednesday at 7.30pm.

CHRISTCHURCH

In Christchurch, there are only two teams in the whole city, one womens and one mens. This means that currently there is no league in christchurch. However they still have trainings and have built a very welcoming community. Christchurch plays in the National Club Championships in Wellington with both teams made it to the finals in 2023.

They are also looking for new players so if you are interested in giving it a go, contact Jenny at jenny@hotmail.co.nz and she will be able to provide you with more information.

DUNEDIN

In Dunedin, there are three teams in the whole city, one womens and two mens. All these teams are a part of the Otago Handball Club. There is no consistent club league in Dunedin, but they still have trainings and have built a very welcoming community. Dunedin also has a school league that schools participate in. Otago Handball plays in the National Club Championships in Wellington with their mens team winning the tournament in 2023.

They are also looking for new players so if you are interested in giving it a go, contact Jenny at jenny@hotmail.co.nz and she will be able to provide you with more information.
